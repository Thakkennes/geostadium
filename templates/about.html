How to Play

1

Choose a League

Select which league you want to play: MLB, Triple-A, Double-A, or other minor leagues. Each league features stadiums from that level of baseball.

2

Find the Stadium

You'll see a team name and league. Navigate the satellite map to find their stadium. Zoom in, pan around, and explore until you've found it.

3

Place Your Marker

Click on the map to place your guess. You can drag the marker to adjust your position before confirming.

4

Score Points

Your score is based on how close your guess is to the actual stadium. Get within the stadium grounds for maximum points!

Scoring

  • Base Score: 5,000 points per round
  • Distance Penalty: -1 point per 10 meters from the stadium
  • Perfect Zone: Guesses within the stadium boundaries get full points
  • Time Bonus: +500 points if you guess within 30 seconds
  • Hints: Using hints reduces your score by 10-15% each

Hints

Stuck? Use hints to help narrow down the location, but each hint costs you points:

  • Country - 10% penalty
  • City - 15% penalty
  • Capacity - 10% penalty
  • Year Opened - 10% penalty

Leagues

  • MLB - All 30 Major League Baseball stadiums
  • Triple-A (AAA) - Top-level minor league stadiums
  • Double-A (AA) - Mid-level minor league stadiums
  • High-A - High Class-A minor league stadiums
  • Low-A - Low Class-A minor league stadiums
  • Spring Training - Spring training facilities in Arizona & Florida
  • Other Sports - Football, basketball, soccer, and more from around the world
